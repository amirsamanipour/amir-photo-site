About Me

I'm Amirhossein Samanipour, a professional photographer with over three years of hands-on experience in portrait, commercial, and outdoor photography. I hold a Comprehensive Digital Photography Certificate from the Karaj Technical Center, along with specialized certifications in Portrait and Commercial/Industrial Photography from Didnegar Academy and the Technical and Vocational Organization of Iran.

I've worked in both studio and natural light settings, solo and with teams. My work has been featured on platforms such as PhotoImagineArt, Limited Art, Eyeland One, and Portrait in Moment.
